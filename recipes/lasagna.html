Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Lasagna pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt & pepper
  1. Boil pasta in lightly salted water.
  2. Mix tomato sauce, garlic, olive oil, and salt & pepper and cook for 15 minutes.
  3. Make layers of pasta, sauce, and cheese.
  4. Bake for 20 minutes on 400 until cheese is bubbling.
